Principal Investigator

Meet the leader of our research group

Dr. John Buckeridge

Senior Lecturer in Thermofluids and Turbomachinery

Dr. John Buckeridge leads our research group with expertise in computational materials science, focusing on defects in semiconductors and energy materials. His work bridges theoretical modeling with practical applications in sustainable technology.

Research Expertise:

  • Density Functional Theory
  • Defect Physics in Semiconductors
  • Energy Materials
  • Machine Learning for Materials
